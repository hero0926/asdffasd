MAUM - You have a right to be happy

Living in a different country as a foreigner and trying to achieve your full potential is hard. Our aim is to enable foreigners to find solutions to their complicated personal situations, to improve their individual skills, to gain knowledge of and orientation in the Korean social and institutional environment. We are determined to help foreigners with social integration into the majority society, and to help them use available resources, networks and services to live normal, stable lives.
